## Adunation

Components sulk in languorous  
entanglement, agglomerates  
from discombobulated junk  
of deprecated provenance.  
  
Disorderly alembics rust,  
antennas jut from orreries,  
forensics-*in-potentia*  
cake tenons stuffed in mortising.  
  
A microcosm overlays  
inconsequential rummaging  
with something convoluted and  
peculiarly discomfiting:  
  
a Gordian bewilderment  
of coxcombing embroidery  
conglomerating cordage with  
its overbraided joinery,  
  
gold manacles, eccentric lathes  
on cantilevered balances,  
electroplated armatures  
to acupressure callouses,  
  
Ambrosian censers (nautiloid—  
or ammonite?-embossed), perfumes  
from (counter-chiral) helicoid  
condensers dreaming ocean blooms,  
  
fresh *Mandragora*, hellebore,  
pressed Calibar-beans, amaranth,  
dried bladderwrack and madrepore,  
*A. bella-donna*, marjoram,  
  
an aging ergot-honeydew-  
and-*rhododendron* hydromel,  
an *amanita*-chamberpot  
and stagnant vase of asphodel,  
  
a diuretic battery’s  
hydraulic sphincter diagrams,  
an unguent phylactery  
with oscillating diaphragms,  
  
commemorative amulets,  
preservative medallions,  
tourist-interpolations of  
apotropaic talismans,  
  
a septenary abacus,  
Platonic dice, an amateur-  
ish *suiseki* audience  
for *hnefatafl* massacres,  
  
wallpaper tesselations’ non-  
Euclidean trajectory,  
haphazard chalkboard pentacles’  
dimensional synecdoche,  
  
a starfield-forecast scatterplot,  
wet risograph chromatograms,  
cyanotypes, stem-cuttings from  
a figurative cladogram,  
  
self-cauterizing bandages  
for trepanation-augury,  
a tourniquet-and-catheter’s  
reversible topology,  
  
vermilion, a regulus  
of antimony, calamine,  
chalcanthum, verdegris, plumbum,  
the keys of Basil Valentine.  
  
Adjacently, a congeries  
of audient ecology:  
formaldehyde remonstrances  
from moribund phylogenies,  
  
dicephalic planarians,  
exsanguinated pelicans,  
a desiccated pangolin,  
pelagic exoskeletons,  
  
the dewclaws of a theropod,  
an octopus’ radula,  
a platypus’ ankle-barbs,  
some narwhal macrodontia,  
  
a makeshift tintinnabulum  
from scrimshawed walrus bacula,  
a spoonbill-jawline speculum,  
an ambergris-filled ampulla,  
  
innumerable diatoms  
entombed within the countertop,  
an undiscovered solifuge  
and customary attercop,  
  
an incomplete homunculus  
(or embryonic basilisk?),  
its incubating pumpkin-flask  
and fructifying catalyst,  
  
the periscoping denizens  
of perforated demijohns,  
aquariums grown redolent  
with negligence and carrion.  
  
And vertebrates-in-analogue,  
exotic animalia  
whose paginations segregate  
symbolic penetralia:  
  
*Mentaculus*, glossed codices—  
accordioning so manicules  
reallocate parenthesis  
to logomantic manuals,  
  
pseudepigraph-apocrypha  
(with ponderous appendices),  
old chronicles of epitaphs’  
prognostical impendency,  
  
ten-thousand-thing compendiums  
(by overspecificity  
pretending to avoidance of  
all anthropocentricity),  
  
graffiti-chalked *omphalos* stones,  
decoded glossolalia,  
the graphite-pocked facsimiles  
of graven latrinalia,  
  
chronologies of fraudulent  
stenographer-anastrophes  
(amanuenses’ apothegm-  
by-maloprop sagacity),  
  
nostalgic reconstructions from  
sectarian anthologies  
already retrojected in  
sedate historiography,  
  
an imprimatur-catalog’s  
edulcorated obsequies,  
corrected proofs for ever-more  
insistent hagiographies,  
  
mosaic parchments honeycombed  
with verso-patches, folios  
eroding into sediment,  
papyrus archipelagos,  
  
some *oshibana* sandwiches:  
the diatonic pentagrams  
of choral incunabula  
on petrified chrysanthemums,  
  
delusionary protocols,  
amorphous marginalia,  
encomiums on folderol…  
assorted miscellania.
